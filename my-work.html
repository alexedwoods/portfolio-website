A little info about Silicon Repairs

What is Silicon Repairs?

Silicon Repairs is an electronics repair business started by me to improve my skills not only with hardware but also with software. It has since grown into a mode of putting my development skills to the test in a business environment.

What have you developed for Silicon Repairs?

I have not only developed a bespoke website but also a backend to support this website where repairs can be managed.

How often do you work on Silicon Repairs?

I tend to work on Silicon Repairs whenever I have free time (usually in the evenings) most of my time is spent fixing devices & interacting with customers.

What would you improve about the website?

I'm currently in the process of overhauling the website to make the site look more modern and facilitate new features such as refresh-less tracking and code clean ups.
